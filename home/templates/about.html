About Us

Welcome to Mittal Bhojanalya, where tradition meets taste! Since 1990, we’ve been serving authentic, homestyle Indian food made with the freshest ingredients and the warmth of true hospitality. Our menu celebrates the flavors of Haryana and North India, offering everything from classic thalis to special seasonal dishes.

Located in the heart of our town, we’re a family-run eatery where every meal is prepared with love and care, just like it’s meant to be. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick bite or planning a family dinner, Mittal Bhojanalya promises a memorable food experience.
